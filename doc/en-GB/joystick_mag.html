Joystick magnitude

Instruction

Description

Gets the magnitude, separation from the center, of the joystick between -100 and 100.

Inputs

  • X-axis Pin: X-axis analog pin.
  • Y-Axis: Y-axis analog pin.

Output

Magnitude of the joystick (value between -100 and 100).

Example

This examples shows how to read the direction and magnitud of a joystick, showing their values on the console.
