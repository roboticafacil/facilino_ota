Read microphone (digital)

Instruction

Description

Returns the value of the digital signal of a microphone (it must include an analog comparator like the microphones included in the Arduino Kit 37 in 1).

Inputs

  • Pin: Digital microphone pin.

Output

Digital reading value.

Example

This example shows the reading value of the digital signal of a microphone by the console (serial port).

All changes will be lost!
