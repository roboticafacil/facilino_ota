Joystick direction

Instruction

Description

Gets the angle or direction of the joystick in degrees. When the joystick is centered the values ​​are not reliable as they may be sensitive to signal noise.

Inputs

  • X-axis Pin: X-axis analog pin.
  • Y-Axis: Y-axis analog pin.

Output

Angle of joystick mast (value between -180 ° and 180 °).

Example

This examples shows how to read the direction and magnitud of a joystick, showing their values on the console.
