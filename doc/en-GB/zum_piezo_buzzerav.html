Generate sound (advanced)

Instruction

Description

Generates a beep sound with the specified frequency and duration.

Inputs

  • Pin: Pin to which the buzzer is connected to.
  • Tone: Frequency to be emitted (in Hertz).
  • Duration: Beep duration (in milliseconds).

Example

In this example, very low and very high-pitched sounds of different durations are emitted.
