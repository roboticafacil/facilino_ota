Sets 'up' and 'down' position of robot arms

Instruction

Description

Sets the 'up' and 'down' positions of the robot arms.

Requirements

Facilino's License.

Inputs

  • Degrees: Position in degrees to be adjusted.

Dropdown lists

  • Arm and position: Determines the arm (left or right) and mode to adjust (up or down).

Example

In this example the angular positions of 'up' and 'down' of the arms are established and a movement demonstration performed by raising and lowering the arms.

All changes will be lost!
