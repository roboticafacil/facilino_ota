Converts to Farenheit degrees

Instruction

Description

Converts a temperature in Celcius degrees into Farenheit degrees.

Requirements

This library requires Dallas Temperature installed on Arduino IDE. This library is available at: https://github.com/milesburton/Arduino-Temperature-Control-Library and also through the Library Manager:

Inputs

  • Temperature: Temperature in Celsius degrees.

Output

Temperature in Fahrenheit degrees.
