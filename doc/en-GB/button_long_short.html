Short or long click with a button

Instruction

Description

Discriminates between a short or long click with a button when pressed.

Inputs

  • Pin: Pin (digital input) to which the button is connected to.

Fields

  • Time for long press: When a button is hold for a longer time, then it will be considered as long-click. Otherwise is a short click.

Statement Instructions

  • Long press: Instructions to be executed in case of pressing the button with a long click.
  • Short press: Instructions to be executeed in case of pressing the button with a short click.

Example

In this example, we print a message on the console discriminating wether the button has been pressed with a short click or long click.
